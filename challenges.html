Our Challenges for you! Post them on our facebook page to show us what you have done!

Williams Challenge

  • Design your own campsite and build a model on the floor using household objects. Or in the garden with sticks and grass. Please take part and post your pictures on here

Dear Future Me – write a postcard to yourself

  • The game leader should give everyone a postcard and ideas for how far in the future they’d like to send their postcard. The players write or draw a note to their future self. Grown ups can help with scribing or spelling.

Fabulous fancy frames

  • Create a frame for a photograph that makes you smile then make a gallery for everyone to admire. Decorate the border of the frame with pens, stamps, stickers and other photos you have taken.

Mark my words - make a bookmark

  • “This activity is a chance to get creative, but it also reminds everyone how to look after books,” the Scouts say. “Bookmarks help people remember where they’ve got to without damaging the book. What other materials could bookmarks be made from?” What Bookmarks can you make at home?

Dance Mat Typing!
Dance Mat Typing

  • The Way I got so fast at typing was using a bbc biteize activity calling Dance Mat! Its a fun way to learn typing skills for kids and adults!

Parachutes!

  • Make your own paratroopers with some string and a plastic bag! Drop your new army from the top of the stairs or a ladder! Whats the heaviest object you can successfully float down?
