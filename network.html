Age 18 - 25 Network

Network is the fifth and final section in scouting. Crouch valley's local & only Network group is Valley Network and it's for scouts between the age of 18-25 years old. We meet every Monday evening & are based at Ark Lane campsite.

What Do We Do? What Makes Us Different to Explorers?

The Network section focuses on community, international & adventure. Our program is youth led and is fairly similar to the Explorer Scout's activities however it can be even more flexible and/or adventurous due to our age range. Our section allows you to continue or start awards like the Queen's Scout Award, the Explorer Belt, the Scout of the World Award & DofE award. We still have regular camps whether is be a National Network Event like Gathering (a wonderful camp in the Lake District) or large international events like the World Scout Moot (2017 was in Iceland) .

Activities Week to Week

Week to week, we may get involved with bowling, climbing, skating, wide games, cooking, walks, the cinema, quizzes etc but where it's youth led, if people have a realistic idea then they can run that evening's activity. Overall, It's a social & relaxed section and you can make it your own.
