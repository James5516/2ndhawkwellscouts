Ages 8 - 10 1/2 Cubs

Cub Scouts are young people aged between 8 and 10 1/2. Our Cub Pack meets on Thursday Evenings. Cubs make a promise, help others, play games, go on outings and visits, go camping, learn about the outdoors, gain badges and create things.

Activities include:
- Camping
- Cooking
- Competitions Againest Other Cub Packs
- Fire Skills
- Games
- Outdoor Skills
